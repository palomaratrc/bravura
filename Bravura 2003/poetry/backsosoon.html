Back So Soon?

Empty promises, now you’re leaving again
You swear you’re gone for good this time
I doubt it though
Is this like the other times?
Should I wait for you to call?
I know just how it will go
It’s a familiar conversation
You ask if I’m seeing someone new,
I say no
I ask the same
You don’t reply
There is a few moments of apologies
We agree to meet
Some old hangout with too many memories
We kiss, and embrace
Make love, and then live happily,
Until the next time.
