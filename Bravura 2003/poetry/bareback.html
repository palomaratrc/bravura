Bareback

You are the wild
The savage and untamed one
Whose thick skin rumbles
Like dry desert clay
Thundering against my thighs
Weighing your heavy bones is
Your thick hide which
I swear I clench with
Every desire in my body
I pull my reddened fingers
Through the knots in your
Wind curled hair
Yet I know
That with each
Torn and leather like breath
You are ready.
Your ambition, the ambiguity
The instinct
Never tangible to me
Is beyond this graveled sand
And you are ready.
You feel the electric power
Sever the earth
And you can run
Far from this caged drought
The sun blazes your eyes
And you know
You can flee
Escape
You are ready.
