Coffee Shop Shared

A coffee and sandwich with barely a word.
Yet together we intertwine in mere glances
Never to be severed by the sharpest of swords.
It is not I that sits in silence, but we that
swim in the sea of placidness.
This is the relationship as we bathe in our aura
Suddenly succumbing to sentious cyclical scenes
Sharing scamingly sexual sects and means.
Enveloping the earth our future ceases
Paranoid particulars and pieces begin to part.
The view, I stare into brown pupils of art.
My love has endured time, life’s rhythmic beats of the heart
No longer exists as we surpassed the start.
Space and time invoked between our souls,
We teach and learn abroad physical imprints printed like burns.
She sits relaxed, simple cup in hand unchanged,
Sealed inside our globe.
All else seems unknown, while in sequence hardships linger.
A pointed finger directed to nothing directed to nothing.
Silent singing robust.
Stares entranced words ringing.
The sound of love in the coffee shop shared.
