The Death of This Life

Drowning in a pool of sorrow, my skin torn off, revealing my inside.
Your piercing look shooting through me.
No time to think or breath.
My heart beats faster now, louder, harder, can’t contain it.
The only sound I hear is your voice tormenting me.
Please wake me up, open my eyes to this nightmare.
When will it be over?
My body falling; wailing with pain and suffering.
This endless grief and hatred claws at my soul, wishing to devour me.
Hold onto something, don’t let it overpower me.
Will this ever pass?
My eyes shut, long to open to see the light.
My body being hurled into an eternity of anguish.
This torment rips the life out of me.
Will I ever be whole again?
