The Eyes of Shame

Suspended in a moment.
Seized by her agony.
Confined in mute desire.
Yearning for that hopeful meaning found only in nameless souls.
Frozen in that ancient time of fate.
Her eyes fade past their shades of plastered smiles.
Liberated from the cage that burned guilt and pain into time forever.
Love is young and endless, never seeing that shame and anguish.
