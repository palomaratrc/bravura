The Inferno Within


The antsy intense fire rages
Deep within and burns its way
Thru my eyes and on the tips of my digits.
Its an anger that is strange to me
One I can't recognize
Don't want to.
It doesn't stop
It grows stronger.
The stronger it gets
The more I think
Its insanity just making a guest appearance.
As I lay there
It vanishes as quickly as it sparked
And I fall asleep
In the exhausted ashes of the fire.
