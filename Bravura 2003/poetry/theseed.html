The Seed


when did i close up
like a peach
keeping the seed
so deep inside
allowing only the adventurer
to find and see
hear and know the depth
of my mind
the color of this heart

when will i reopen
the sweet door
invite in light and
covet my fears no more

sunlight deliver me
soften and undo my
chinile cave and
release the dance
that like a prickley top
will spin free.
