Which Remains

The soft places where
My induced evil had
Secured the jagged edges
Of its teeth
Are faded scars which have
Since melted into the tones
Of my own flesh
They fade, yet like so many
Times before, my heart
Falls inside the silence
Of anticipation, knowing such
Answers exist beyond
Vision and the only
Strength
I’ve ever been sure of
Is my love for you
Chills spill across me
Because at any moment
I would mangle the scabs
Of all which remains
Just to hold you again.
