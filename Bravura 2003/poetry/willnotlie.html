Will Not Lie

 

Come ask me why and I will say,
"Please ask again some other day."
Against my will I wake again
Dreading sunshine and all the pain
I hold my breath one minute, two
No hope at all, I'm still with you
Tugging at my shame and sorrow
Back at it, same time, tomorrow
The sun's hot rays pierce through my soul
I'm blessed with life, but for a toll
One million people, maybe more
Have come at some point to my door
"Don't be bizarre," they ask of me
I smile and nod with evil glee
If life's bitterness I must now swallow,
In turn for not becoming shallow,
I gladly gulp it down and sigh
"Why not?" I ask, I will not lie.
