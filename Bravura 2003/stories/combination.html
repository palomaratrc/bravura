Combination Monologue #1

     Kick the soapbox aside as today loud and clean will not be enough. Clear your throat into the spotlight, and if you might be speaking to yourself then your pedestal might as well be personalized. Step up onto a stack of smut magazines piled high on the toilet seat.

***

     Why is misogyny, the hatred of women, so very like scatology, the study of or in this case the obsession with, feces? Apart from the general unpleasantness associated with both terms, those who regard women with hostility are often preoccupied with the same issues that can bring all things fecal into a kind of comic if not spiritual reverence. These people, the misogynists and the scatologists, are normally not women, however, they are normally not men either; they are both lingering boys.

     Freud once stated that, particularly to the dreaming mind, gold is shit, symbolically. By this connection are men who shower their women with jewelry in fact trying to subjugate if not entirely degrade them? If so, then it could also be suggested that a man literally defecating on a woman actually means to lavish wealth and reverence on her. Despite what Freud knew, it brings to mind the dynamic between socializations of women as delicate icons to be worshiped or beasts of burden to be enslaved. Through separation of the sexes many men become fixated on these ideals long before puberty’s new emotions can challenge or violently invert their perceptions. Upholding the truth or damning the lie of either extreme becomes a man’s testament to what they asserted during childhood. This is often one of many ways to further pamper the boy who wasn’t ready to grow up.

     And on that same note of pampering, we can wipe up another of life’s earliest lessons. Bodily discharges are associated with poor hygiene, thus bad manners, thus they are the first and most easily accessible weapons of rebellion. Before children learn about sex, every joke and insult revolves around toilet and the harnessing of gasses they’ve been taught to excuse or keep subdued. Boys soon find they can gain power with a pull of a finger even when they cannot get it with the strike of a fist. One long laugh can erase a hundred scowls, and as men grow jaded or overwhelmed with ‘grownup jokes’ they frequently fall back on those familiar yet always personalized stenches. This humor is at the base, at the beginning, and thus always at the top, a willful smile for the shocked or disgusted faces and all the miserable growing up they represent.

     The real Peter Pan would have flown around with a little flap open in the back for extra propulsion and even a bomb or two. He’d have never let Wendy join him, whether to keep her out of danger or to keep her on the ground. If there is still any doubt on the common origin of misogyny and scatology, just look at how men still express their anger or contempt. All curses, with exception to the spiritual ones, revolve around either bodily function or reproductive dysfunction. It is no great surprise that the ‘s’ word and the ‘f’ remain our dirtiest. The toilet shines as man’s first triumph just as the bedroom remains man’s most enduring challenge, and he must either work within society’s rules for them or dwell on the memory of when he could make his own. And perhaps, when impotent and in diapers, these men might remember their wives and mothers more fondly.
