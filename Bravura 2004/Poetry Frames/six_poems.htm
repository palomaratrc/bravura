Six Poems
by Emit Levart
 Second Place Winner, Poetry Category

 

DAY DREAMIN'

Wings extended,
I coast on warm thoughts.

 

HALLOWEEN RESURRECTION

I ran out of masks
When I ran into you.

 

SHAKESPEARE EYES

If I could speak
In Poetry,
Your eyes would be
My Words.

 

LOVE STEW

I've got a
Cupid hair
In my soup.
It tastes good.

 

DIVORCE #1

He talks in black and white,
She sees in rainbows.

 

ODE TO CONCRETE

In a world of
Black and white stoplights,
Numbed by dull gray rules,
They dream of shadows.
