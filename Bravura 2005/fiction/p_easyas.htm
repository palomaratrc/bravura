Easy As...
Amanda Panella

     You know those people that say they just sort of “fell” into their profession? Yeah, you know whom I’m talking about. Those people, the ones that litter the late-night television shows while you sit on your couch with potato chip crumbs on your shirt that was once white…hmmm…I should do my laundry. The people you stare vacantly at, your eyes just seem to fixate on them and you can still hear the faucet dripping. I’ll have to get that fixed. Those people make me angry. I get so angry at them because they sit there in their fancy clothes with their hair glistening and teeth white as the God-given snow (quite unlike that of my shirt) and they tell us that their current project is so great and it’s the best they’ve ever seen. Funny…they said that the last thirteen times they were on the show. I remember because once she wore a pink blouse…it was her first time on the show, a nobody at the time…now she’s being paid a hell of a lot just to flaunt those breasts on camera. I think she’s a chicken just for doing it. Fantastic. We watch these people and have that love-hate relationship with them. They capture you and draw you so close to them that you can see that mole on his chin. Yuck, I’ m not a fan of moles. He’s got some makeup on it at least. It’s like a flesh colored bump now – flesh colored bumps – this is what fascinates us? I don’t understand our society sometimes. Why do I sit here night after night after night…I’m just looking at these people, listening to them market this current thing that they’re doing to me so that I’ll rush out and support them. Not this time! No sir-ee! It’s just not going to happen. They’ve suckered me in so many times and every time I’m disappointed with them.
     Do you think that maybe it hurts to smile so much? Ooo…she’s got a lump of Vaseline on her teeth. Ha-ha. She deserves it…maybe that’ll teach the m not to do close-ups. If they’re trying to make these people seem appealing, they’re not roping me on in. Whatever happened to test patterns? Maybe I could actually fall asleep that way and not be glued to this drivel. Trash!!!! It’s all trash! Just look at me, a dreamy-eyed moron with my eyes glossed over. Oh they hurt. I’ll bet they’re all red now. Where’s my Visine? Forget it, I’ m not getting up. How can I miss a single second of this? This garbage that I constantly need and crave…I blame my insomnia really. Seventeen months…that’s a long time for no sleep. Well, lets be honest – I do get sleep. An hour and a half average every forty-eight hours is about it. Look at them prancing about. Ugh, how degrading. They are a spectacle of gargantuan proportions – what a life. I should have such a life! If they can do it, anyone can! That’s what they claim anyway – of course I don’t quite believe it myself. But who knows? Maybe I’m wrong. No, of course not. I’m right, they’re wrong. It’s never as easy as it looks, if it were, everyone could do it! What about the people like me? I know I can’t do it, and every time they make it seem so easy – it’s all a damn lie. There are five things I can do well: 1. Brush crumbs onto the floor, 2. Breathe, 3. Sit, 4. Eat potato chips and 5. Watch the T.V. An exciting life I do lead! Not as exciting as theirs I’m sure. Is it jealousy that ravages over me? I suppose I could help them make a buck by spending one of mine. But why?! Why should I?
     Fabulous, now my stomach is upset. My potato chips foil me yet again! Make that four things I can do well. Where’s my Pepto? Sweet, sweet delicious liquid that soothes – come to me my lovely! There it is. Take a swig and feel better. All well again. I think that I love these people because they entertain – the enthusiasm, the fact that they lead a better life than I do – it makes me feel that there’s hope for me too. But I loathe them as well – because they made something of their life and I did not! I’m wrecked with grief. Torn between two feelings like a star-crossed lover…our relationship being this box comprised of liquid crystals and plastic and…not like lovers at all. Friends I suppose is what we really are. We’re not friends…we’re enemies! No…frenemies. That’s the word! Where’s my dictionary? Right, I don’t own one. Oh well. Look at her eyes…they’re as vacant as mine are. No they’re not – quit lying to yourself. I really should get some sleep – but wait! It’s only three payments of $39.95? How can I lose?! I mean, even someone like me can use it – after all, they did say it was as easy as 1…2…3.
