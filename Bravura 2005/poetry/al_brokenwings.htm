Broken Wings
Hana Al-Iesa
She is like an angel with broken wings,
she has lost her will to fly.
Her life so filled with emptiness,
she is living in a lie.
She has lost the hope
that once made her so happy and so free,
her mind so full of forgotten dreams
that will never come to be.
Once she thrived with family
and a place she could call home,
now she exhists in despair,
forever she is alone.
With her head upon her knee,
she heaves a morbid sigh.
Trapped she whispers.
Trapped she screams.
Trapped until I die.
