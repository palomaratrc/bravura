Ghost Stories
Tim Batiuk
Yellow nerve gas shades the poison from every masked man with two heads. They dangle cures that end our lives. (They steal the coins off from our eyes.) And my ten thousand fathers sing to me:

"The TV news is only ghost stories, so trust that which you touch. But don't grow up a skeptic-your open mind means much too much. Hold on tight to your judgment! We sensationalize our fear! But don't yet rule out werewolves, or we have no magic here. "
