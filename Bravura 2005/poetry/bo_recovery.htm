Recovery
Donna Booher
This journey has not ended; I know the countless steps to go,
And to this I am one feather, fluttering fast against the throw.
Though my resolve seems brittle, I feel it's nerve more than before,
If I surrender never, we'll toil together and endure.
I cannot count the pieces of all my broken guarantees,
To what cannot be mended, I pray that time can remedy.
To those I've introduced to fire produced in my own hell,
I will not disregard your scars and you of mine, as well.
Though my will may play the healer, it cannot carry me alone,
I will need the strength of others when I am lacking of my own.
The winds will change and drive the clouds to again reveal the sun,
So that one feather may find its place with those who've fought, and won.
