Closed-minded
Holly Foster
Colors and emotions blend into a haze
A foggy light unseen by your fearful eyes.
Wrapped in the pains within your soul
While taking lives with each breath.

Trying to hide the horror within the lies
While demonstrating false love
Through your undeveloped mind
Neglecting the pain within.

Generating ideas that don't exist
Manipulating the living,
Through charm and belief.
Disguising normality with jealousy

Forever lying to the empty heart
That drives madly through the dark.
Ripping at the threads of love
Forgetting all that's been done.

Ending on a somber note
Before you take the one,
Trying to fathom her decisions
And marking her with blood.
