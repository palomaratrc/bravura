Inferior Divinities
Marissa Houle
In the broken space between night and day,
The angels fly to and fro.
Beneath the foamy sleets of spray,
The daemons wait for the time to go.

They'll never know for whom they cry,
As guns bark and leaders lie.
Those that fired not with alloy, but with hate,
Knew not that in the air above a 7th level war was waged.

As the angels sang before the guns,
There was an utter stony silence.

The warriors rested and heavenly combatants waited.
The crooked silence shattered only by a universal sigh.
