In Our Infancy
Peter D. Jones and Sherrie Gonzales-Kolb
A whirling vortex looms out of the void
in a fusion of essences
traveling along
invisible, electrically-charged synapses
compelling us inward...outward...upward...onward
in a ghostly fiction twisting through both time and space
One particle
Many minds
It binds uncertainly… frees incessantly…. Hides…
Blushing in reverence to possibility...
cloaked behind the intensity
of our nudity...
far more profound than physicality…
Which is easier to expose?
our hearts? or our Souls?
And why?
Earth-mother laughs at such childish questions,
For All is open
And All is closed
In a playful paradigmatic puzzle.
Because the world turns not by our rules,
but by the innocent
invincibility of the inner-child...
who remains
immortal and unafraid.
