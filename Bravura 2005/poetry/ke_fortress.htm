Fortress
Elayne Ketcher

I used to write poetry. Now it seems like long ago and far
away since my heart was young.

Seasons ago, my words made love to the paper. Now I find
only teardrops where the ink used to flow.

I’ve allowed spectators to view my soul, as if in some
hideous carnival freak show.

I’ll seal them deep inside myself for preservation in the
fortress of my heart.

Yes, there was a time when…

I used to write poetry.
