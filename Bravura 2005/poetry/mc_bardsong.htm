Bard Song #6
Mikey McMath
Of the valley low,
and mountain high,
the fate of heroes
you cannot lie,
though life is righteous
it still doth fly
even heroes die.

We remember our fallen,
we long for them nigh,
legend never forgets
a hero gone by,
but mortal minds
do run dry,
and even heroes die.

The gift of life,
a fleeting sigh,
we watch it leave,
we say goodbye,
with all restraint,
try not to cry,
because even heroes die.
