Two Poems
George Pearson
Cam World

Help me speak
In this world of surveillance
"We are watching you."

My Trojan Horse

Pillow soft blows
Penetrating bark
Disguised so fitfully
With smiles and hugs

Held hands and laughed
So joyous and friendly
Disguised so fitfully
It came so quickly

It came in the dark
Daggers in my heart
Crushing my kingdom
Penetrated by her Trojan Horse
