Collapse
Whitney Pope
My body caves in
to more efficiently show you
the vital organs
that remained hidden for months
perhaps years.
My skin
is the same.
Big enough to swallow
us both
and make us
supermodel thin,
big enough to fold back
over heaping mounds
of fatty tissue and muscle
to protect my heart,
my stomach,
my lungs
from your pillaging hands
that tried before
to steal them from me
and succeeded.
