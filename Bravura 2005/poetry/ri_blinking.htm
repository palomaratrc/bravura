Blinking
Travis Rivas
It's mocking you
The ceiling light reflects
off the blank white screen
frustration forever
burned behind your eyes

Every time it blinks
a snapshot of your face
Eyelids heavy retaining weight
from the six pack you drank
to inspire creativity

Sandpaper on your face remains
too busy for its ritualistic removal
Your lips move around the words
"Fuck" and "You"
every time it disappears and reappears

Coming back again to say
You still haven't written anything
I'm still blank
and there's not a damn thing you can do about it

Still it blinks
No, not blinking
a wink
a pompous wink with a
crooked smile
and a cherry tongue pointed at you

Hate
Fear
Annoyance
All things enter your mind
except creativity
another wink
another face
another curse

Finally your fingers dance
Not an inspired dance
A spiteful one
that will take eyeballs from sockets
that will knock teeth from smiles
that will rip tongues from mouths
and what follows next
is beauty
