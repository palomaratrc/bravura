“Back in the Day”
Brian Payne


We liked to feed the ants.
When we were kids we
Would clean the pool and feed
The crispy corpses of drowned insects
To our hungry colony of ants near the shed.
One week they didn’t come out
Of their tiny hole to devour the limp spider.

Daddy said they were pests.
Sissy said they were pets.
We never brought it up again.
Now we throw the centipedes and
Scorpions to the dust in the alley
When we find them in the filters.
