Getting Laid - On My Terms
By Sherrie Gonzales-Kolb


You were an ubiquitously competent lover
all encompassing,
well, almost -
mind and body -
the Soul?
I'll reserve judgment for
that hypothetical day which remains to be seen
but -
never underestimate
pure, carnal pleasure unto
frenzied release
as a starting place
where all masks and bets are temporarily off
and I invite you to be under my skin
somewhere in between raunch and routine,
tidbits of CNN, World Updates and Rogaine commercials
in this tawdry hotel
that reeks of cigarettes,
cheap one nighters, and
desperate moments -
no Gideons' in sight -
where we will not wake up
in each others' arms -
leaving before check out
as brazenly as we came
back to our discreet and orderly lives.
