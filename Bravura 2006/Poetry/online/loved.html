Loved
By Nicole Lee


from where I kept hold,
from the weeds on the side of the cliff,
from the exposed tree roots push away         push         away
from the narrow, precipitous footholds force away,
backwards into the downwards of anchorless drift

goes whispered persuasion,
        Let go
                Let go
                        Let go
to ease my grip on the cliff
readying me to joyfully slip
loose into romantic freefall.

have faith in unpracticed flight
go the whispers. have faith
the ground won't hurt when you reach it,
of course i'll be there, waiting;
just remember to breathe as I dip in
to you...go the whispers as I rip in
to you. tears are natural go the
whispers

I do not know if you want
me crying or torn
but it's just us:
I believe, it’s just us
enmeshed, entwined, divine.

go your whispers soft, warm,
        girl, my girl
        Let go
                Let go
                        Let go

And somewhere, behind my own
echo, I hear another girl moaning
in response to your same soft tone.

        Go the whispers
                to all of your girls
                        a chorus
        Go the whispers
        the widely distributed farce
                Love
        /trust me
                                Love
        /its only you (and you) (and you)
                                Love
                                         /love
                                                /love

continue the whispers
        as girl after girl
                        after girl
                hits the ground, impact unbroken,
                                rapture tattered.
