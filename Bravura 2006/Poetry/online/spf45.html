SPF 45
By Sherrie Gonzales-Kolb


I soak up the sun
slathered in SPF 45
and in my pink visor
content to be without you on this
'made for lovers only' day
I am affirmed by how well
you know me, and yet, how you
are still surprised at the hybrid fruit
produced by my mercurial moods
delighted and humbled by my gifts
of fire, wood, and words
wrapped in string
and when I get home tonight
there will be a card from Mama
like the one I've received every year
of my life
something lacey and girlie and
astonishingly unlike me
and it will tug at my heartstrings
like they have for the past 44 years
until I toss it in the trash
with a love that could envelop
the entire world
my son will bring me lunch
and some little chocolate candies
shaped like seashells
which he knows I will not eat, and
which he knows I will give to someone
who will be sad today
on this day
made for lovers
I silently and solitarily absorb the warmth
of this most unselfish and reliable sun
through all the layers of the protection
that I may always
find necessary
knowing that despite all of the glaring imperfections
in this life
I am loved.
