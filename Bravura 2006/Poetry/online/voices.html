Voices
By Michael Parker


The war torn faces
Distended and screaming
Pleading
Not here; Not now; but Everywhere;
Hands trembling; blood soaked fear
It’s all for nothing, and nothing for all;
Innocence lost, consumed by sand
No one knows, No one cares
What we’ve lost in blank stares

A last breath taken, all is lost in that split second
Should I shoot or should I hide
Mom will be so proud today
I gave my life for the USA…

Oh, he was so handsome then
What a waste
To say goodbye so soon
My son
I shall weep, and forever keep
This token flag upon my chair
A tribute to you, for what you’ve done

Podium ready, here I stand
To tell the land our important plan
We shall fight, and we shall win
At a cost I know, It’s not cheap
To keep my house so bleached and clean
Sons and Daughters they will fall
But it’s for the good of us all.
