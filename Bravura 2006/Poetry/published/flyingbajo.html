Flying Bajo, Flying Alto
By America B. Feldman


Flying bajo leaves without tree
Flamboyant desires,
Partiendo during night,
Little birds in the dark,
En silencio, without roads,
Volando without breath
Reaching nueva vida…
This life that was dinied
There…was in black

Into cold wind,
Con frío en el alma
Thirsty dry tears…
Far away from home
Without tierra
Buscando ese cielo
The unfinding gold
There… behind their lands
Vuelo alto into the sun
There behind their lands,
Sin vida…empty paths

Nada easy amigo,
Sin desmayo, or dismal
Flying high looking dreams…
Solo sueños…unclear sueños
Finding any side para vivir
Silent sanctuary…
Any scar, any rock,
Ninguna canción
Nothing can stop el vuelo…
Flying fast sin parar
Then one day come back again
Maybe alone, or quizá con Dios

Far way the sweet voice,
En el viento into the echo
The Mother Tierra calls…
Silent cry…silent souls,
Sin sentir, neither hear
Solo con el corazón
Flying high, not slow anymore low
Vuelo alto…no despacio
Arriving to the sky…
Then maybe one day come back,
There… mamá land always estará
