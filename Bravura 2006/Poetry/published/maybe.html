“Maybe”
by Ashleigh Blake


Maybe it’s her eyes that sparkle different colors
In different lights.
Maybe it’s the roses in her hand
That deliver her emotions
In endless opportunities
For love.
Maybe it’s the way she smiles
At the memories
She frames around her room.
Maybe it’s the Louis Vuitton accessories
She drowns her loneliness in.
Maybe it’s her arms that wrap around
Anything that reaches out to hold her.
It could be her name,
Or her face…
But it’s what we don’t know
About her
That we pretend we do.
