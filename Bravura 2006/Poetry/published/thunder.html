Thunder
By Jennifer Hickerson


Bright light fills the room
The explosion of thunder
Shaking windows
Jostled into awakedness
Frantic searching
Once warm blankets, now
Cold desert sand
Dust and detonations
Filled the cold air
Shouts and screams
Of friend and foe alike
Another explosion
Of light and sound
Frantic searching
A missing rifle
And only a dozing wife
To bring him back to reality
To sleep once again
