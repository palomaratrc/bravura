You Almost Had Me Fooled
By Jodi Sanchez


While a couple
tried to conduct me
to a threesome
in an unfamiliar city
I thought you stood me up.

You were forty five minutes late.
Given our roller
coaster relationship,
you almost had me fooled,
and these freaky strangers
almost had me convinced.

Ready to cab it
back to the airport,
I suddenly saw you.
A sweaty blonde,
thin silver frames
sliding down your
crooked nose;
white Hanes tee
clinging to your
Ivory soap skin.
You were dashing
through the crowds
of the Boston train station
and I knew
I wouldn't have
to dine
alone.
