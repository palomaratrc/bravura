If you are having difficulty reading the text on the Bravura web pages you may adjust the size of the text by taking the following steps:

 

For those who are using Microsoft’s Internet Explorer click on the View menu in the menu bar at the top of your screen.  Move the mouse to the Text Size category; this will produce a sub-menu with text size choices listed.  Click on the size text you wish to use.

 

 

If you are using the Firefox web browser you can adjust the text size by also going to the View menu in the menu bar at the top of the screen.  Within this menu is a Text Size category.  The sub-menu that appears when you place your mouse over this category allows you to adjust the size of your text by clicking on either the Increase or Decrease size choices. You can also use the keyboard by holding down the Control Key (Ctrl) and then pressing the ‘+’ key or the ‘-’ key to increase or decrease the text size respectively.
