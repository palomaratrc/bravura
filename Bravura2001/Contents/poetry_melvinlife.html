Life

by Cat Melvin

 

Love is all I am.

Love is the bridge

between where you are

and

where I am.

I am the bridge.

I am Love.

I am.

 

 

Richness, spinning by.

Me, hungrily opening up

to gulp.

Life running down my chin.

Tantalizing tastes, colors, feels

pouring through.

That is mine.

I am that.

Again.

 

 

Reach out, touch life.

Now.

Today.

All her treasures,

her sights,

scents,

touches.

Be.

Be.
