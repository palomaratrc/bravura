Patti’s Journey

by Cat Melvin

 

My journey spans a period of time

but I am timeless, Eternal.

A whirling kaleidoscope of experience—

winning,

losing,

flowing,

getting stuck,

locked up, tied down, buffeted

by the winds of fate

then

soaring, accepting, rising above.

Life is sweet—eat dessert first.

The universe is a fun place,

full of swirls of life,

bright points of joy,

soft, comfortable days,

cold, rainy nights alone.

We are all its children

riding a roller coaster of experience,

of life,

rising to peaks of cool realization,

swooping down into fiery places

where desire—

for love,

for knowledge—

burns everlasting

or consumes itself, fulfils itself.

I live, therefore I AM.
