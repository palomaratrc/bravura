In The Back Seat
Nicole Nelson-Hicks

In the back seat,
The car door handle gets tangled
In the deep auburn curls of my hair.
I am rooted to the spot.
He kisses me, covering my mouth
As he slowly plucks away
One layer of clothing
At a time.
My mind flies away
To a field of green
Where I have suddenly become
The tender petals
Of a shamrock.
He loves me-
He loves me not.
