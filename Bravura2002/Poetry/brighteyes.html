Bright Eyes
Anthony Darius Moreno

Bright eyes
my baby’s got-
soft lips,
so sweet & hot.
An interesting intellectual,
she juggles her sentences-
keeping you guessing
like warmth in the shade.
A great butt
my honey has-
dancer legs,
so strong & supple,
an eclipsing ecclesiast
she loves her creator
honestly hoping, to
have us all saved.
Beautiful hair
my eve enjoys-
small hands,
so electric and perfect.
An amazing adorer
like rivers to lakes flow,
her love into me goes-
Illuminating like filament.
