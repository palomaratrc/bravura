If only a moment
Brian Ramuno

Sticking straw through
Painted clouds as
Birds rest upon
A gentle sun
Wind whispers a
Soft tone through
Day and night falling
With grace.
Sky apart from
Earth has wandered
Too far.
Waves crash with ease
Sun touches the ground
If only a moment in time.
This gift beyond
All thought taken
With ease.
Clouds keep moving
Birds keep flying
Sand will wash
Away all hope
Till tides bring
A new start.
And again
We swim
If only a moment
In time.
