Bravura, Palomar College's literary magazine, has published an array of literary works and provocative images by students for over thirty years. Bravura reflects our commitment to celebrating the diversity of creative voices at Palomar College and our ongoing desire to enhance the cultural life of our community.

 -The Bravura Staff
