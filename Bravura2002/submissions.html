Bravura is published annually each spring by the Palomar College English Department. Submit your short stories, poetry, non-fiction, commentaries, essays, literary works and black and white photos and art January through early March. Please limit your sumission to 10 - 15 pages. All work should include an electronic copy on disc.

Submit work to the Bravura Box in the English Department or to the mailboxes of either Dr. Carlton Smith or Dr. Rocco Versaci.  Also, work may be submitted via email to rversaci@palomar.edu.  No work will be returned.
