The Bravura is HERE!

 
  Bravura

 
Since 1964, the Bravura literary journal has existed 
at Palomar College as a forum for the best of student 
writing and artwork. Published each spring, Bravura 
is assembled by student editors who complete all of the 
work associated with creating a journal and its 
companion website. Consider becoming a part of this 
award-winning tradition at Palomar College by either 
submitting your creative work or by signing up for 
English 137 (the class that puts the Bravura together).
