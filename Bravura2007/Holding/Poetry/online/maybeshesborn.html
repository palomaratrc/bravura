maybe she’s born with it
By Nicole Lee


fish
cute little fish
in glass
breathing liquid,
extracting gas,
cute little girl
in the usual dress
seeping joy
in a messy sorta
frenetic sorta
desperate sorta
way

she was never
meant to fit a
pinafore just cause
that's what's always
come before.
leaking joy,
seeping through,
like every girl
untrained will do

breaking past
the cover-up, she finds
blushes never come
in compacts to be
applied with brushes—
they are the result of
early morning adrenaline
rushes

sweet fish
cute in a bowl
lovely woman
messy and whole
smelly, stained
and wonderfully
drained of emptiness

her delicacy cracks
in the living
