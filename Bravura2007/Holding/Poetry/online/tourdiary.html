Tour Diary
By George Pearson


I wish I could have come
to the place
where you were
standing in the photograph
with all the pedestrians
they were wandering around
I was wondering just
where you were at
cause there were no signs
planted in the ground
no subtle clues
as to where
your feet were touching down
and I think
I just wanted to be there too
I just wanted to touch your face
if I left now
could I get there soon?
cause I just want to be with you.
