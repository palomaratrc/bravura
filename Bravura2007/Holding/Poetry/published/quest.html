Quest for a Parking Pass, or, A Quick Note to the C.P.
By Tanna Klein


Went I, with currency green,
to pay the yellow box obscene.
T'was there I then discovered
one whose wide mouth was covered
and one other with open jaw
that swallowed naught put in its maw.
Still, they both had mouths petit
which, perhaps, would coinage eat...
Traipsed I back to building yon
to alchemize paper to metal therein.
My task complete, I journeyed again
to the faraway place where to boxes do den,
there to find the first resolutely aslumber
and the second with no appetite for coins of any number.
Fed up quite fully at last
did I trudge to this stead and bid you that,
should you care for my dollars, two,
you pay me a visit in yon classroom.
