It’s not all about time,
Some things are timeless.
It’s not all about money,
Some things are priceless.

A gift impossible to repay.

It's not all about work,
Some people are jobless.
It's not all about staying home on a cloudy day,
Or sleeping in that extra hour.

Some people are homeless.

It's not all about age,
Whatever's “cool,” the next big thing, and the latest rage.
Some things are ageless, endless, and
It's not all about you.

Or me.

It’s not all about friends,
Some people are friendless.
It's not all about looks,
Some people are faceless.

And some people just can't see.
