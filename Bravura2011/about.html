bra•vu•ra (br vyeor' ;brä veo' rä) a daring display of brilliant skill

Bravura is an annual publication of Palomar College featuring fiction, poetry, graphic art, and imagery Bravura seeks outstanding work by students attending Palomar College.

Established in 1959, Bravura is
an award winning literary magazine featuring writing by the Palomar College community. Exploring a variety of genres-from fiction and poetry to graphic art and rap-the journal provides a forum for  emergent talent in the San Diego  area. Many writers and artists  featured in the journal have gone  on to publish work elsewhere  after publishing in the journal.

  In this challenging age for all   literary endeavors, particularly   journals, Bravura benefits from   continued support from a variety   of sources. While the journal is   produced by the English   Department at Palomar College,   it would not exist without the   following funders:

 The President's Association
 The Students of Palomar College
 Donations

Thank you for your continued support.
