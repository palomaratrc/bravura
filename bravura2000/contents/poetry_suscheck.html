Dirty Girls
By C.A. Suscheck

 

Get up late girls, paint your face girls,

Pull up, zip down, low cut

Clean on outside

For awhile girls,

Musky perfume girls,

Never say no girls,

Me girls, more than you girls ---

Dangerous

Hot times, high highs, (low lows.)

Picture girls.

Quantity

Back and forth girls,

Spin around girls, do the dance girls,

Couples only, who's alone?

Show me, show you, show all,

Eyes that squint,

Secrets to give girls,

Snake tongue smile girls,

Take your soul, dumb your mind.

Touch me, touch there, anywhere,

Anytime,

See me, want me?

I'm "good,"

Need me, that's right,

This is life girls, all for love girls,

It comes first girls

Set the standard, way it should be.
